# @octanejs/nuqs

## 0.1.0

### Minor Changes

- Initial release: nuqs 2.9.1 ported to the octane renderer. Vendors the
  framework-agnostic core (parsers, serializer, loader, standard-schema, update
  queues) verbatim and reimplements `useQueryState`, `useQueryStates`, and the
  React/custom/testing adapters on octane's hooks.
